About

Independent Wheel specializes in replacement wheel components for Kinesis, Fikse, and HRE wheels. The company was founded by Thatcher Shultz in 2008, when he realized how difficult it was to source 3-piece wheel components for motorsport wheel brands. Independent Wheel has locations in New York City and Washington State. Contact us for any inquiries.
